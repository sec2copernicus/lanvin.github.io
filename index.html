Pantai Mangrove


Preventing Beach Erosion Mangrove forests are one of the places that can guard the border between land and sea areas.

Mangrove Beach is a popular destination that presents views of mangrove forests and swamp habitats in one coastal area.

1. Chips/cookies If you find a Bruguera gymnorrhiza mangrove, this mangrove can be processed into food, such as chips or pastries. Because the fruit of this type of mangrove has a more friendly taste on the tongue.

2. Syrup Sonneratia or Pedada/Bogem mangroves can also be processed into syrup. The aroma of this pedada is usually fragrant and has a fresh sour taste. Usually those who like to process pedada into syrup are in the Rungkut area, Surabaya.
